## Layer 1 — Company Profile Analysis

### MAP Consulting (Management Action Programs)

MAP is a **65-year-old performance improvement consultancy** built around its proprietary MAP Management System™ — an accountability operating system for business leaders. It has served over 15,000 organizations and 170,000 leaders across financial services, healthcare, technology, nonprofits, real estate, and manufacturing.

**What works:**
- Deep institutional credibility — six decades of delivery
- Consultant model built on former CEO practitioners, not theorists
- Accountability system focus creates sticky client relationships
- Vertical diversity provides broad market access

**What the FRED analysis flagged:**
- No publicly confirmed AI integration into the MAP Management System™
- No named AI product, AI advisory offering, or AI governance posture as of April 2026
- Website and public positioning unchanged from pre-AI consulting era
- No evidence of a dedicated AI or MLOps capability within the consulting bench

**MOC Assessment:** MAP is operating a legacy system in an AI-accelerating market without a visible transition plan. This is not a fatal condition — it is a **structural gap that creates an advisory entry point**.

### Vistage Worldwide

Vistage is the **world's largest CEO peer advisory and executive coaching organization**, with 45,000 members across 40 countries, $126.8M in annual revenue, and 1,300 trained Chairs facilitating monthly peer groups. Their model targets SMB CEOs at $5M+ revenue and has 65+ years of proven member growth outcomes — Dun & Bradstreet data shows Vistage member companies grow **2.2x faster** than non-members.

**What works:**
- Dominant installed base — 45,000 CEOs is a near-unassailable network moat
- Launched AI Sales Leader Certification program (Q4 2025)
- Acquisition activity signals growth intent (acquired firm October 2025)
- CEO Confidence Index gives them a live data signal on SMB sentiment

**What the FRED analysis flagged:**
- AI certification is sales-focused, not governance-focused — this is surface-level AI integration
- No public AI accountability architecture in their member advisory model
- Compliance-as-Cover (EBT v2.6 Pathway 3) confirmed: certifications cited as governance evidence without underlying accountability design
- AI ROI measurement methodology: **UNMEASURED** — 45,000 member firms with no public AI outcome tracking framework

**MOC Assessment:** Vistage is the scale threat to MAP and the **partner opportunity for Epoch**. They have the network. They do not have the accountability architecture. That is the gap Epoch fills.
