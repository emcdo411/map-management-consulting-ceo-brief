# ⚙️ Decision Trigger Map

![Layer](https://img.shields.io/badge/layer-Governance-red)

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Define **when action is required**

---

## 🔑 Trigger Types

### 1. Threshold Breach
- KPI deviation  
- Performance drop  

---

### 2. Pattern Detection
- Repeated failure  
- Behavioral drift  

---

### 3. Time-Based
- Missed deadlines  
- Stalled progress  

---

## ⚠️ Risk Without Triggers

- Insights ignored  
- Delayed action  
- False accountability  

---

## ✅ Outcome

Every insight must answer:

- Who acts  
- When they act  
- What defines success  

---

> No trigger = no execution
